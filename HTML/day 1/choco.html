Chocolate chips cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2large eggs
  • 2 spoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cup choco chips

Instructions

        1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). 
        2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter ,  white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
        3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract. 
        4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; ->Actually,  combine the flour, baking soda, and salt in a seperate bowl
        5. fold in the chocolate chips (use your favorate brand)
        6. Drop spooful of dough onto ungreased baking sheets (for extra crispiness )
        7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden  brown ( check every 2 mins for perfection )
        8. allow cookies to cool on the baking sheer for 5 minutes before transfering to a wire rack.
